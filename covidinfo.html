COVID-19 Information

Learn more about the virus and how to prevent its spread

What Is it?

COVID-19 is caused by a novel coronavirus called SARS-CoV-2. Symptoms may show up 2-14 days after exposure to the virus in the form of a fever, coughing, and shortness of breath (not limited to this). The virus is highly contagious and is mainly spread from person to person through close contact with other people and through respiratory droplets (coughing, sneezing, talking). However, the virus may also spread even if people are not showing symptoms (asymptomatic) and by touching objects or surfaces that have the virus on them. (https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/symptoms-testing/symptoms.html)
