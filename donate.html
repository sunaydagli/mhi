Donate now

We need your help to provide masks to healthcare professionals and to anyone who needs a mask. We appreciate every cent.

Your donation goes directly towards personal protective equipment (PPE) to be delivered to large hospitals, local urgent care clinics, communities hit hard by the pandemic, or anyone who needs a mask to safely achieve their goals. We accept monetary donations to help us purchase PPE or the PPE itself. Any donation is appreciated!

We graciously accept surgical masks (unopened), N95 masks, hand sanitizer and/or wipes (minimum 70% alcohol), safety goggles, disposable gloves, or protective face shields to donate to hospitals. Please contact us if you want to donate PPE!

Our current ways to submit monetary donations are GoFundMe or Venmo. Please see below to donate directly or go to their site. Thank you for your contribution in defeating COVID-19!
