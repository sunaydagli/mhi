Defeating COVID-19 Together

After the transition of power to the Biden administration, many are hopeful that the carnage brought about by the COVID-19 pandemic will cease in coming months. However, despite new efforts of mass vaccine distribution, hospitals are still at max-capacity and struggling to find resources to treat all of their COVID-infected patients.

As the pandemic remains an ongoing issue in the United States that impacts every facet of our lives, Masked Heroes Initiative plans to continue the effort of donating masks and other PPE to hospitals and other centers in-need of supplies.

With your help, we can make sure that people can stay safer while making a collective effort to save lives and move past the pandemic.

Masked Heroes Initiative at a Glance

A little bit about us

Our Message

No matter what your background or who you are, we are all at risk to this virus. We must all come together to defeat COVID-19, and no one should get left behind.

Our Mission

We are here to help during this crisis by providing masks to the frontline healthcare workers mitigating this outbreak and anyone who does not have a mask to protect others.

Our work

From large university hospitals to local urgent care centers, we provide supplies to organizations running out of supplies in cities suffering from outbreaks. Sometimes, we even provide to areas that haven’t seen an outbreak — so that they can be prepared for one.

Our Team

Our team is a group of high school and college students, chipping in to do our part in overcoming the COVID-19 pandemic crisis together.
